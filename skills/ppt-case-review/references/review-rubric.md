# PPT Case Review Rubric

## Two Scores

### Case Sharing Quality

Use this score for whether the deck can teach others something useful.

| Dimension | What To Check |
|---|---|
| Story clarity | Can the audience understand the customer, problem, actions, and result? |
| Scenario specificity | Are concrete business scenes shown instead of abstract slogans? |
| Method transferability | Can another CSM or region reuse the method? |
| Evidence and examples | Are claims backed by cases, data, screenshots, or customer feedback? |
| Memorability | Does the deck contain a clear concept, formula, or phrase? |

### Management Reporting Quality

Use this score for whether the deck supports management decisions.

| Dimension | What To Check |
|---|---|
| Business closure | Renewal, expansion, NRR, adoption, risk reduction, or other business result. |
| Data rigor | Consistent口径, traceable numbers, clear time range, unified units. |
| Attribution | Does the deck explain why results happened, not just what happened? |
| Strategic altitude | Does it connect to team capability, regional replication, or operating model? |
| Actionability | Can management decide what to keep, improve, add, or stop? |

## Severity Examples

### P0

- Metric conflict across slides that changes the conclusion.
- Factual error about customer, product, timeline, or result.
- Serious typo in customer name, key number, or core conclusion.
- Logic hard failure, such as result appearing before action without explanation.
- Compliance or privacy risk.

### P1

- Same metric uses different口径 without explanation.
- Page role is unclear in the storyline.
- Claims are plausible but unsupported by evidence.
- Methodology is named but not operationalized.
- Important stakeholder or customer expectation is missing.

### P2

- Wording can be sharper.
- Visual hierarchy can improve.
- A stronger transition sentence is needed.
- A reusable concept can be named more memorably.
- Supplementary examples would make the point stronger.

## Comparison Dimensions

When reviewing multiple cases, compare:

- Narrative structure.
- Methodology depth.
- Data quantification.
- Scenario granularity.
- Commercial closure.
- Strategic altitude.
- Reusability as regional or team playbook.

Use one sentence to name each case's unique position, for example:

- “从0到1建立能力”
- “从1到100推动运营”
- “从问题解决走向经营闭环”
- “从工具使用走向组织协同”
